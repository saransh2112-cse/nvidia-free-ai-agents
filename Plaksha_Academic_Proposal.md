### 🌟 Vision
The goal of this workshop is to provide Plaksha B.Tech students with an elite, industry-standard understanding of **Agentic AI Engineering.** We will move beyond simple prompting and teach students how to architect autonomous swarms that can research, debate, and visualize data without human intervention.

### 🎯 Why This Workshop?
1. **Industry Expertise**: Bringing direct experience from building the industry's first Travel AI Copilot at HRS Group.
2. **Cutting-Edge Tech**: Utilizing NVIDIA NIM and Llama 3.1—the same stack used by enterprise AI teams.
3. **Open Source Pedigree**: Students will finish the day by contributing to a public, open-source repository, providing them with a verifiable "Portfolio Win."
